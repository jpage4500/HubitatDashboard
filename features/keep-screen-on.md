# Stay Awake

**Where**: navigation menu -> More Settings.. -> Stay Awake

This setting allows you to keep your phone or tablet awake (screen ON). The following options are available:

* Always
* Between _Start Time_ and _End Time_
* Disabled

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (231).png" alt="" width="188"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

### Always

* This option will keep the screen ON as long as the app is running and in the foreground

### Between _Start Time_ and _End Time_

* This option lets you select an ON time and an OFF time. The screen will stay ON between these times and be allowed to sleep outside of these times
* There's some additional settings as well with this option:
  * On/Off Time (1): primary on/off time (ie: 8 AM - 10 PM)
  * On/Off Time (2): **optional** secondary on/off time (ie: 5 PM - 10 PM).&#x20;
    * use case: if you want the screen to be on in the morning (ie: 5 AM - 10 AM) and then have it off until you get home from work
  * Screensaver:
    * set to 0 to allow the display to turn off outside of the on/off times (default)
    * number of seconds before the app will enter [screensaver mode](keep-screen-on.md#screensaver); only applies when outside of the on/off times
* ![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (84).png>)

### Disabled

* This option will not keep the device screen on (the OS system display settings will apply here)
